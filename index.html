About Us

Founded by Ladakhi filmmaker Stanzin Jigmet, we’re not outsiders dropping in for a shoot—we’re locals who have lived this land, its culture, and its soul. While Ladakh has become a popular location for filmmakers because of its dramatic landscapes, only a local storyteller can reveal its true depth; our intimate understanding of its traditions, faith, quiet moments, and everyday struggles lets us craft films with emotion and authenticity to the screen in a way no one else can.

End‑to‑End Production

From concept to the final delivery — all handled entirely in‑house.

Altitude‑Proven Crew & Gear

No rentals or outsiders needed. We operate with cinema‑grade lights, primes and Steadicam at altitude.

Cultural Integrity

As Ladakhis, we portray the region with truth, not tourist‑filtered clichés.

Weather‑Resilient

We can film year‑round in sub‑zero temperatures, high passes, frozen lakes or remote villages.
