ITI Markhur

Client: UT Ladakh Administration

Creative Approach: To raise awareness and increase ITI enrolments, we used humour and relatable storytelling. The film shows how skill‑based courses can turn raw creativity into job‑ready abilities, empowering youth beyond reliance on government jobs.

Overview: A short awareness film encouraging students to pursue skill‑based learning. It follows a young student who devises an innovative solution to a daily problem that spirals into chaos, illustrating the need for proper training to refine great ideas.
